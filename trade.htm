We’ll Buy Your Car

Are you looking to sell your ride but not sure how much you can get? Then visit our website, and we’ll value your trade. We make the process of
valuing your trade as quick and easy as possible, and we always offer fair deals. If its time to sell your car, truck, or SUV, our Norwood dealership is the
place to be.

How Selling Your Car Works

While selling your vehicle may seem a little overwhelming, at our dealership, the process couldn’t be simpler. We have an easy three-step process, and
that process includes:

Filling Out Our Online Form

The first step in selling your vehicle to our requires you to fill out our online form. This form will ask for information about your vehicle, like its
year,model, and kilometers

Having Your Vehicle Appraised

Next, we’ll need to assess your vehicle by performing an onsite appraisal. Having your vehicle appraised is necessary to make sure the information you
provided was accurate and to determine if your vehicle has any damage or if it has any aftermarket accessories.

Receiving An Offer On Your Sedan, SUV, Or Truck

Finally, once we’ve completed our appraisal and know your vehicle’s worth, we’ll then make an offer. At this point, Boston area customers can accept the
offer we make and leave with a check in hand, or they can use their vehicle’s value toward the down payment of a new Car.

Trade-In Benefits

When trading in a car to our dealership, the biggest benefit Newton and Quincy residents will receive is being able to decide what to do with their
vehicle. Sellers have options available like using their old vehicle’s value toward the down payment for a new car or even a used model, or they can leave
with money in hand. Trading in always guarantees that residents of Providence, RI, will receive a fair price no matter their ride.
